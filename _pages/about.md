I received my Ph.D. degree in mathematics from the University of Science and Technology of China, Hefei, Anhui, P. R. China, in 2021. From 2022 to 2025, he held a postdoctoral position with the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Ben-Gurion University of the Negev. I am currently an Associate Professor with the Institute of Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Sciences, Xidian University, Xi'an 710126, China. My research interests include combinatorics, coding theory, and their interactions.

## Research Interests

- Coding theory
- Deletion-correcting codes
- DNA data storage
- Constrained coding
